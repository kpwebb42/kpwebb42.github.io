Dues Statements / Closing Letters

If you need a dues statement / closing letter / current invoice from the PFHOA due to refinancing your mortgage or selling your property, please fill out the form below. There is a $10.00 fee to generate the letter, which in most cases is paid through the closing of the refinancing or selling of the property. If your agent will not make this payment, you may pay online or by check.

Online - Please note that there is a $1 convenience fee associated with making a payment online.

Check - Make check out to: Prairie Fields HOA. You can deposit it in the PF HOA Drop Box on Prairie Rose Lane or send it to PO Box 478, Savoy IL 61874.
